Bachelor Thesis

Title: An auto-catalytic reaction-diffusion equation’s numerical analysis

Supervisor: Associate Professor, János Karátson

Description: A reaction-diffusion equation is a special partial differential equation which models the concentration of the chemical substances in a reaction. The equation that I am analyzing is in a steady state. It was mentioned in a mathematics book which was published in the middle of the 80’s. My analysis is about in which case can the equation be solved and I am trying to approximate the solution with finite element method. I’ve implemented the solution analyzed the results in Matlab. My thesis in Hungarian.
